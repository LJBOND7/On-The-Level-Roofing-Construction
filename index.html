Our Story

A name this area already trusts, back where it belongs.

In 1985, Larry's father hung out a shingle that said On The Level. The name meant two things, and he meant both. The work would be square, plumb, and done right. And he would be straight with every customer, no games and no surprises. For years that is exactly how he ran it, and it is how the name earned its reputation across these neighborhoods.

Over time he shifted his focus to another company, All American, and On The Level quietly stepped back. The name went dormant, but the standard behind it never did.

Now his son Larry is bringing On The Level back to serve the same families and the same markets. Same standards. Same honesty. A new generation holding the level. If you trusted the name before, you will recognize the work. If you are meeting us for the first time, you are getting decades of family construction experience and a promise to keep things on the level.
